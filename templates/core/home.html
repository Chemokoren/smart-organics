About Smart Organics

Empowering Smallholder Farmers Across Kenya

Smart Organics Limited is a Kenyan agribusiness company established in {{ company_year }}, transforming smallholder agriculture through sustainable practices.

Headquartered in Eldoret, we work closely with smallholder farmers, farmer groups, cooperatives, and agricultural development partners to improve farm productivity and strengthen rural livelihoods. Our integrated agricultural solutions enable farmers to transition from subsistence farming to commercially viable agribusiness enterprises.
