Who We Are

Smart Organics Limited is a Kenyan agribusiness company established in 2015 and incorporated in 2019 under the Companies Act, 2015 as a Private Limited Company.

The company was founded with the vision of transforming smallholder agriculture through sustainable farming practices, access to quality agricultural inputs, and structured market opportunities. Headquartered in Eldoret, Uasin Gishu County, we work closely with smallholder farmers, farmer groups, cooperatives, and agricultural development partners.

Through innovation, partnerships, and farmer-centered solutions, Smart Organics Limited contributes to the development of a more productive, profitable, and sustainable agricultural sector.

Our Mission

To provide farmers with high-quality organic agricultural inputs, certified seedlings, and professional agronomic support that enhance productivity, sustainability, and environmental conservation.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted provider of sustainable agricultural solutions, empowering farmers to build profitable and resilient farming enterprises.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Smart Organics.

Sustainability

Promoting environmentally responsible farming practices that protect our soils, water, and ecosystems for future generations.

Integrity

Operating with transparency, honesty, and accountability in every interaction with farmers, partners, and stakeholders.

Farmer Empowerment

Supporting farmers with knowledge, resources, and opportunities to transition from subsistence farming to commercial enterprises.

Innovation

Continuously improving agricultural solutions and services through research, technology, and modern farming practices.

Quality Excellence

Delivering reliable and high-quality agricultural products and services that farmers can depend on season after season.

Our Promise

Healthy Soil • Wealthy Farmers • Better Life

This is more than a tagline — it's our commitment to every farmer we serve.
